Short Description

A button for manual emergency alarm activation. Its flexible wireless design makes it easy to install in hallways and escape routes, with a signal transmission range of up to 100m.

Detailed Description

In situations where a fire is discovered by individuals before smoke or heat reaches the automatic sensor system, the SafeFire HM2EB-W Manual Call Point is highly effective. With just one emergency press, the signal is transmitted wirelessly (up to 100m in open areas) to immediately trigger the entire strobe siren system.

The button operates on a battery with a lifespan of up to 3 years, allowing for easy wall installation anywhere without the need for complicated drilling or wiring. Multiple call points can be strategically placed throughout a building for rapid fire alarm activation.

Key Features:
  • Manual emergency alarm activation button
  • Wireless RF transmission up to 100m
  • Multi-function design for flexible placement
  • Long battery life: 3 years
  • Easy wall installation without wiring
  • Compact design (62.4mm x 14.3mm)
  • Rapid system activation capability
  • 24-month warranty with 1-to-1 replacement
