Short Description

An ultra-loud 95dB alarm siren with a flashing strobe light, ensuring clear evacuation commands. Connection range up to 200m. Features a built-in backup battery to maintain operation even during power outages.

Detailed Description

To ensure everyone in the area receives the emergency evacuation signal, the SafeFire HM2WD-W Strobe Siren is an indispensable protective device. The product emits a piercing 95dB warning sound (measured at 1m) combined with a continuously flashing strobe.

It receives wireless RF interconnected signals from sensors within an operating radius of up to 200m (in open areas). Notably, despite utilizing AC mains power, the siren is equipped with a built-in Lithium backup battery (720mAh), ensuring the fire alarm system is never paralyzed even if the fire causes a power grid failure.

Key Features:
